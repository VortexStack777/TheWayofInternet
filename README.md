### **FAQ's**

**Q. What is this index?**  
**A:** This wiki comprises all the major wikis. The implementation of the wiki is designed in such a way that it does not feel cluttered or bloated. We will auto-update it on a regular basis. We consider this our home for browsing the internet.

**Q. What is so different about this wiki that stands out from others?**  
**A:** This wiki mainly focuses on removing any kind of personal bias while strictly emphasizing reliability, performance, and user experience. This is achieved through criteria-based ranking. These criteria are constantly evaluated and modified to provide the best experience for users.

**Q. So there will be no favored entries?**  
**A:** No. We do have entries that are custom-curated by our wiki editors and marked with a special icon. These entries are valued and respected by our wiki editors.

**Q. How do I contribute?**  
**A:** We would be pleased to receive help from the community. Please join our Discord for further directions.

**Q. Do you host the content?**  
**A:** We neither host the content nor seek credit for the entries. We are working on this for a specific reason: to help users find things online more efficiently without wasting time and effort researching them.
